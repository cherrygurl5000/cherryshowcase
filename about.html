Hey, my name is Stephanie and I’m an Electronic Engineer with a passion for Front End Development. Currently, I’m looking for a permanent position in the Web Development World where I can continue to utilize the skills that I have learned.

My main focus has been on PHP, HTML, SCSS/CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, Git, and SQL. Prototyping and wireframing have been completed using Justinmind Prototyper, Mockplus Prototyper, and Canva Design. From there I complete the vision using my web design acumen.

After being exposed to coding while earning my Electrical Engineering degree, I did not take an immediate interest in it. I began learning more about coding as a hobby and grew a fondness for learning the languages.

Most of my focus has been on CSS images, but I also enjoy working with people to help their vision come to life. The thing I enjoyed most about engineering was creating a product from beginning to end and ensuring that the client was happy with their final product. I have been able to feel that same sense of ownership in producing scalable code for my clients and their businesses. I take pride in my products and I enjoy exceeding the expectations of my customers.

Coding makes me happy. When I’m having a bad day, I can work on a mini coding project and genuinely be at peace. I’ll work on the same issue for hours at a time and still be interested in finding different ways to solve it. I’ll start with an end time in mind, then find that hours have passed and I have to force myself to stop. Working with code is what I truly enjoy doing on a regular basis. I hope that I am able to work with you and surpass your expectancies.

Please check out some of my projects and my resume. And if you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me or to check out my LinkedIn page .

Thanks for your time, and I look forward to hearing from you!
